About

I'm a Machine Learning Scientist at Apple, where I work on Siri's voice trigger and speaker recognition systems. I build and deploy on-device ML models across every Apple platform — scaling personalized voice recognition to 40+ languages worldwide. My current work is proprietary — it spans model training, evaluation infrastructure, fairness research, and deployment across six Apple platforms.

I hold a degree in Symbolic Systems with an AI concentration from Stanford University, where I published research on detecting workplace sexism and leveraging computer vision to predict livelihood indicators in developing regions.

Previously at PayPal, I secured a patent for a sequence modeling approach to predict and autoscale server resources, and founded the company's internal Machine Learning Guild.

Python · PyTorch · On-Device ML · Speaker Recognition · NLP · Computer Vision · Model Evaluation & Deployment · Fairness in ML

My work is driven by a commitment to applying cutting-edge AI to create more impactful, intuitive, and equitable user experiences.

When I'm not training models, I'm playing guitar, listening to music, watching Stade Rennais FC, and using oxford commas.
